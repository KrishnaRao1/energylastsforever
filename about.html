What does Energy Lasts Forever mean?

Energy Lasts Forever is all the time and effort we as humans put into our life's passions and work. This energy contributes to things in our world that will remain after we leave it.

The Goal

Krishna Rao's mission is to create a community of hard working individuals dedicated to their subjects and passions. Don't be driven by rewards and recognition. Just work hard, because ENERGY LASTS FOREVER.
